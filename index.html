How To Play

How To Play:

The aim of the game is to score as many points in 1 minute (60 seconds) as possible. The game consists of a cube, of which one surface is visable, the surface contains 9 tiles. The cube can be turned by swiping a finger across the screen. This is achieved by tapping any tile on the visible surface of a cube. When a tile is tapped, all other squares on any surface of the cube that are connected by a flat side. Your score for each tap is the number of tiles cleared squared.
Good Luck.
